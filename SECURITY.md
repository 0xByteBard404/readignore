# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The readignore project takes security bugs seriously. We appreciate your efforts
to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge
your contributions.

**Please do NOT open a public GitHub issue to report security vulnerabilities.**

Instead, report them **privately** by email to the maintainer at
**240610626+0xByteBard404@users.noreply.github.com**. Please include:

- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
- Steps to reproduce the issue (proof-of-concept, screenshots, or logs).
- The affected version(s) or commit(s), if known.
- Any suggested remediation, if you have one.

## Response Timeline

We aim to acknowledge receipt of your report within **72 hours** and to provide
an initial assessment within **7 days**. A fix or mitigation will be released as
soon as practical, depending on severity and complexity, and you will be kept
informed of progress.

## Scope

This policy applies to the latest released version of `readignore` on the
`main` branch. Vulnerabilities in dependencies should be reported upstream to
their respective maintainers; please also let us know so we can track and
update affected versions promptly.

## Safe Harbor

We will not take legal action against security researchers who, in good faith,
discover and report vulnerabilities in accordance with this policy. We ask, in
return, that you:

- Give us reasonable time to investigate and remediate before any public
  disclosure.
- Avoid accessing, modifying, or destroying data that does not belong to you.
- Do not degrade service availability for other users.

Thank you for helping keep readignore and its users safe.
